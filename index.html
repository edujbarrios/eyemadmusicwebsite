Biography

Eduardo José Barrios García, professionally known as EyeMad, is an accomplished composer and music producer hailing from Tenerife, Spain. From a young age, he exhibited a profound interest in the multifaceted landscape of music, particularly inspired by the emotionally resonant melodies of early 21st-century Spanish Pop and Rock music. This inspiration guided his creative journey, as he sought to imbue his compositions with similar emotional depth and intricate sound design.

Driven by an intrinsic passion for musical expression, EyeMad undertook formal training in guitar and music theory. Leveraging these foundational skills, he has since cultivated a unique musical voice, pushing the boundaries of conventional genres to create an audibly distinct and emotionally resonant sonic palette.

EyeMad's career trajectory can be characterized as a continual quest for musical innovation. With a commitment to artistic evolution, he consistently seeks to update and modernize existing soundscapes. His innovative approach has positioned him as a burgeoning talent in a diverse array of genres, including but not limited to Reggaeton, Dancehall, Progressive House, and Deep House. As he continues to forge his path in the music industry, EyeMad promises to be a transformative figure for years to come.
